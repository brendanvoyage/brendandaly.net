Welcome



After studying conducting and music history at Harvard, along with a specialization in Romance languages, I embarked on a solo singing career. Boston University and the Longy School of Music of Bard College helped me focus my musical passion on opera performance, through two additional graduate degrees and four years of study. I then traveled across the country and internationally, honing my skills as a musician and apprenticing myself to a new art, stagecraft.

I draw on this wealth of experience from the academic and performance sides of music--plus the third dimension of theatre--in my dynamic teaching method. My deep experience learning five foreign languages also greatly influences my work.

I now teach languages and music as a language, to students aged 4 to 70+. Everyone learns in a unique and special way. And I feel my far-ranging voyage of discovery into the realms of music, language, and theatre has prepared me to be an ideal instructor for the beginner or advanced learner--at the keys, at the mic, or on the opera or musical theatre stage.
